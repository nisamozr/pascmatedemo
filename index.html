In writing, students begin by learning letters, then words, and finally sentences. In time, students learn how to write a paragraph by taking those sentences and organizing them around a common topic. Learning how to write a paragraph can be challenging since it requires knowing how to write a great topic sentence, using supporting details and transitional words, as well as finding a strong concluding sentence. In fiction, writing a paragraph means understanding which ideas go together and where a new paragraph should begin. At Time4Writing.com, you’ll find plenty of resources to help students learn how to write a paragraph as well as improve their paragraph writing skills, including free writing resources on topic sentences and the different types of paragraphs, such as descriptive, expository, and narrative. Articles will assist you in guiding your students and the activities allow them to practice their skills using printable worksheets and quizzes, video lessons, and interactive games. For further instruction on how to write a paragraph, eight-week writing courses are available for all levels.
